Connect Four!

Play Game

Description

  • Player must select start button on screen. To make a move , hover over the top row.
  • Game then initializes Player One and Player Two and their colors.
  • Player One makes first move. Moves consist of selecting a cell on the grid for their disc to be dropped into.
  • Both players take turns until a win condition or a draw is met.
  • When one of the conditions is met , a win or draw message is shown along with an option to restart the game.
